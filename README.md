### Overview
Go Read is an offline reading application designed with user privacy as a top priority. Users can download books within the app and read them offline.

### Information We Collect
Go Read **does not collect any personal or non-personal data**.

Specifically, the app:
- Does not require account creation
- Does not request email addresses or passwords
- Does not collect IP addresses
- Does not track user behavior
- Does not use analytics, ads, or third-party SDKs

### How the App Works
- Books downloaded within the app are stored directly on the user's device.
- The reading experience occurs entirely offline.
- No reading habits or preferences are sent to any external servers.

### Data Usage and Sharing
Since no data is collected:
- No data is processed
- No data is stored
- No data is shared with third parties
- No advertising or tracking is performed

### Data Security
Because Go Read does not collect or store any user data, there is no risk of data leakage or unauthorized access through the app.

### User Rights
As no personal information is collected, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. If you have any questions, you may contact us via email.

### Commitment to Child Safety
BKStudio is committed to providing a safe and secure environment for all users, including children. Go Read is designed as a simple, offline reading tool that can be used safely by children.

### Scope
This policy applies to all users of the Go Read application, including users under the age of 18.

### Content and Interaction Safety
Go Read:
- Contains no user-generated content
- Does not include chat, messaging, or social features
- Does not show advertisements
- Does not link to external websites

This ensures children are not exposed to harmful or inappropriate content.

### Data Collection from Children
Go Read does **not collect personal information from children**, including those under the age of 13.

The app complies with:
- Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
