Overview
Go Read is a book reading application designed with user privacy as a top priority. The app allows users to download and read books directly without requiring any registration or personal data entry.

Information We Collect
Go Read does not collect, store, or share any personal or non-personal data. Specifically, the app:

Does not require account creation or log-in

Does not request email addresses, names, or passwords

Does not track user behavior or reading habits

Does not use analytics tools, advertisements, or third-party tracking SDKs

How the App Works
The app connects to the internet solely to download the books chosen by the user.

No personal data or user identifiers are sent to external servers during this process.

Downloaded books are stored and read locally on the user’s device.

Data Usage and Sharing
Since no data is collected:

No data is processed or stored on our servers.

No data is shared with or sold to third parties.

No advertising, profiling, or tracking is performed.

Data Security
Because Go Read does not collect or store any user data, there is no risk of data leakage, data breaches, or unauthorized access to personal information through the app.

User Rights
As no personal information is collected, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. If you have any questions regarding your privacy, you may contact us via email.
